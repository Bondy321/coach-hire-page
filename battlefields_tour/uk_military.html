Trip Highlights

Cumbria Museum of Military Life

Set within the grounds of Carlisle Castle, this museum offers a fascinating look at more than 300 years of local military history, with uniforms, medals, weapons and personal stories bringing the past to life.

National Memorial Arboretum

Spread across 150 acres with over 400 memorials, this peaceful and beautifully kept site offers a moving place to reflect, with highlights including the Armed Forces Memorial and the daily act of remembrance.

RAF Museum Cosford

Discover over 70 aircraft, including Britain’s three iconic V Bombers displayed together, alongside standout exhibits such as the oldest surviving Spitfire, Bravo November and the National Cold War Exhibition.

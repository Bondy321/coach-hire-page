Highlights & Hotels

Harry Potter Studio Tour

Step into the magical world of Harry Potter with a visit to the Warner Bros. Studio Tour London. Perfect for fans of all ages, this unique experience offers an unforgettable journey behind the scenes of one of the most successful film franchises in history.

Located just outside London, you will explore authentic sets, props, and costumes. Walk through the iconic Great Hall, stroll down Diagon Alley, and visit the Forbidden Forest. See how special effects brought creatures like Dobby to life, and marvel at intricate models such as the breathtaking Hogwarts Castle. Guests can also try out wizarding skills with green-screen experiences or enjoy a frothy Butterbeer!

Hampton by Hilton, High Wycombe

Hampton by Hilton High Wycombe offers the perfect blend of comfort and convenience. This modern hotel features stylish, well-appointed rooms with comfortable beds, free Wi-Fi, and flat-screen TVs to ensure a relaxing stay. Fitness enthusiasts can take advantage of the on-site gym, or relax with a drink in the comfortable lounge and bar area.

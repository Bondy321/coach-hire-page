Tour Highlights

Discover Amsterdam

Renowned for its picturesque canals and vibrant culture, Amsterdam offers a rich tapestry of experiences. Take a canal cruise, stroll through the Jordaan district, or visit world-famous sites like the Van Gogh Museum.

Life On Board & Dining

Enjoy an easy overnight crossing with DFDS. The ship features bars, live music, shops, and a cinema to keep you entertained. Your accommodation is an inside twin cabin (bunk beds).

Meals are not included as standard, but you can add the meal package for just £74pp. This includes an all-you-can-eat dinner buffet and breakfast on both the outward and return sailings.

Life Onboard Ambience

For 40 nights, Ambience will be your home from home. With space for 1,400 guests, she offers a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Dining options include five restaurants, such as the main Buckingham Restaurant and the more casual Borough Market buffet, plus speciality venues like Saffron and Chef’s Table.

When it comes to entertainment, you'll be spoilt for choice. Enjoy West End-style shows in The Palladium, live music in The Observatory, or a film in the cinema. There’s a swimming pool and two hot tubs on deck, plus a gym, wellness centre and salon. Whether you're looking to unwind, stay active, or get involved in a craft workshop, life at sea offers something for everyone.

Caribbean Highlights

Havana, Cuba: Iconic, colourful, and full of character. Classic 1950s cars cruise past colonial mansions while live music spills onto cobbled streets. Explore Old Havana (UNESCO) or sip a mojito in Hemingway’s favourite bar.

Ocho Rios, Jamaica: Laid-back and vibrant. Climb the famous Dunn’s River Falls or enjoy the reggae beats and jerk chicken stalls. It offers a fun, tropical slice of Jamaica that’s hard not to fall in love with.

Harrogate Bargain Hunt Experience

Spend the day with BBC TV antiques expert and entertaining presenter David Harper, well known from hit shows like Antiques Road Trip and Bargain Hunt.

This is a hands-on, laughter-filled experience set in one of Harrogate’s best loved vintage spots, 'Antique on High'. Begin with David’s expert insights and entertaining anecdotes, then put your skills to the test in a lively two-hour bargain hunt challenge. As you search for hidden gems, David will share a wealth of fascinating, little-known stories tied to the antiques you may uncover.

Afternoon Tea at the Crown Hotel

After uncovering hidden gems, the experience continues at the imposing 300-year-old Crown Hotel. Here, you will relax over a well-deserved afternoon tea while David appraises your discoveries and shares more expert insights. Blending antique treasures with Victorian elegance, this entertaining day promises laughter, learning and lasting memories.

The Cedar Court Hotel, Harrogate

Located in the heart of historic Harrogate, the 4-star Cedar Court Hotel offers amazing accommodation overlooking 200 acres of pristine parkland. Dating back to 1671 and sitting on the site of the town's first-ever hotel, this property combines heritage charm with modern comfort. It is only a quick stroll away from some of Harrogate's must-see sights, making it the perfect base for your stay.

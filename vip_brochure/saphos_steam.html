Saphos Steam Train Experience

Venture back to the golden age of travel aboard the exquisite Saphos Train. You’ll be welcomed with a glass of Buck’s Fizz before indulging in a sumptuous full English or fish breakfast. On your return journey, enjoy a champagne reception and canapés, followed by an exquisite five-course seasonal dinner paired with the Sommelier’s choice of wine, cheese, and petits fours.

Hotel Indigo, Chester

Your base for this special break is the four-star Hotel Indigo in Chester, a stylish and modern hotel that combines comfort with character. Inspired by the city’s rich history, the interiors are thoughtfully designed to offer a warm atmosphere. Each bedroom features plush bedding, air conditioning, and a smart TV, while the contemporary restaurant is the perfect place to unwind.

Arley Hall & Gardens

Step into the timeless elegance of Arley Hall, the cherished family home of the 11th Viscount Ashbrook and a filming location for Peaky Blinders. Exclusively opened for our group, this private tour includes both the grand Hall and its award-winning 15 acres of gardens—renowned as some of the finest in Europe. A guided experience with the Head Gardener will bring the estate’s rich heritage to life.

Dalemain Mansion

Explore Dalemain, a Grade I listed stately home in the Lake District with roots tracing back to Saxon times. Admire fine interiors featuring original Chippendale furniture and rare hand-painted Chinese wallpaper. Outside, the award-winning gardens burst with seasonal colour, featuring heritage apple trees and over 200 rose varieties.

Stadium Tour & Legend Q&A

This is a dream day out for any Liverpool fan. Step behind the scenes at Anfield with access to iconic areas including the press room, dressing rooms, the famous This is Anfield sign, the dugout, and the legendary Kop Stand. You’ll also have time to explore the club’s immersive museum.

What makes this truly special is the exclusive Q&A session with a true Liverpool legend. Previous hosts include Phil Neal, Jan Molby, Phil Thompson, and Alan Kennedy—each bringing their own humour and insight from their time at the club.

Liverpool City Football Tour

This unique guided tour takes you through the heart of Liverpool, shining a light on the city’s deep football roots. You’ll travel through areas rich in history, with stops including the Hillsborough Memorial, the location of Bill Shankly’s iconic St George’s Hall speech, and Everton Brow. It’s an atmospheric experience, layered with crowd sound effects and guided commentary from experts who know and love the city.

The Royal Clifton Hotel

Overlooking the seafront, the Royal Clifton Hotel in Southport is full of character and charm. It offers comfortable en-suite bedrooms and a welcoming atmosphere—the perfect base to relax and swap stories after an unforgettable day.

The Belmond British Pullman Experience

Step aboard the Belmond British Pullman and relive the golden age of travel. Your journey begins at London Victoria with a sparkling Bellini welcome, followed by a three-course brunch as you glide toward Winchester.

The return journey is equally indulgent, featuring a champagne reception and an exquisite four-course dinner paired with wine, cheeseboard, and petits fours. With top-class service and beautifully restored art deco carriages, this is a day to savour.

London Eye & Windsor Castle

Experience London from new perspectives with a rotation on the London Eye and a scenic cruise along the River Thames, offering panoramic views of the city's iconic landmarks.

You will also visit Windsor Castle, the oldest and largest inhabited castle in the world. Explore the magnificent State Apartments, St George's Chapel, and Queen Mary's Dolls' House in this stunning royal residence.

Oatlands Park Hotel

Set within 10 acres of beautiful grounds, the Grade II-listed Oatlands Park Hotel is a stunning 18th-century mansion steeped in royal history. Guests can enjoy the tranquil gardens and dine in the stylish restaurant, which serves delicious dishes complemented by picturesque views.

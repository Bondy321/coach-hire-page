Leeds Castle - Private Tour

Visitors never forget their first breathtaking vision of Leeds Castle, rising majestically from its moat and heralded as the “loveliest castle in the world”. You will traverse secret passageways, step into the 1930s boudoirs of Lady Baillie, and immerse yourself in the grandeur of this “Castle of Queens” on a private guided tour.

Before the castle opens to the public, you’ll delve into the 500-acre gardens, meandering through vibrant blooms and tackling the famous yew maze with its underground grotto.

Leeds Castle Stable Courtyard

The Stable Courtyard at Leeds Castle offers charming, individually designed en-suite bedrooms set within the peaceful castle grounds. These sought-after rooms are beautifully furnished to a high standard with luxury toiletries.

A unique highlight is the exclusive evening access to the historic gardens after day visitors have left. You will enjoy dinner and a full English breakfast in the 17th-century Castle View Restaurant, boasting stunning views of the castle illuminated at night.

Penshurst Place & Gardens

Weave your way around the opulent state halls of Penshurst Place, a medieval masterpiece with walls adorned with treasures. This was once the hunting ground of Henry VIII and the birthplace of Elizabethan poet Sir Philip Sidney. Don't miss the breathtaking Baron's Hall, highlighted as one of the world’s grandest rooms.

Option 1: Grandstand Enclosure

The Grandstand ticket provides access to the Tented Village Enclosure, purposely built for the Saturday. Enjoy views of the track, various food and drink outlets, betting facilities, and a live band during the race. Please Note: This ticket only allows access to the Grandstand Enclosure and does not include access to the Grandstand Premier or Parade Ring.

Option 2: Grandstand Premier

For a luxurious experience, the Grandstand Premier Enclosure ticket allows access to the external Grandstand area overlooking the track, as well as the Ayrshire Suite on the ground floor of the Princess Royal Stand with its multiple bars and cafes. You will also have access to the Parade Ring. Please Note: Premier ticket holders do not gain entry to the separate Tented Village.

Option 3: Club Enclosure

Situated close to the winning post, the Club Enclosure offers an exclusive environment with the best vantage points. Enjoy access to the Princess Royal Suite with panoramic views and the Champagne Lawns adjacent to the Parade Ring. This option combines the exhilaration of world-class racing with an atmosphere of elegance and exclusivity.

Important Information

The Coral Scottish Grand National at Ayr Racecourse is a premier event in the British racing calendar, featuring a historic 4-mile steeplechase. Please be aware that this event is strictly for those aged over 18. A valid ID may be required upon entry to the racecourse.

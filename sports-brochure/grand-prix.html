Silverstone Circuit 4 Days

The Ultimate British Motorsport Experience

Experience the thrill of Formula 1 with an unforgettable trip to Silverstone. Nestled in the heart of the British countryside, Silverstone is one of the most iconic circuits in the F1 calendar. Whether you're a die-hard racing fan or simply seeking an exhilarating weekend, this adventure is the perfect way to soak in the atmosphere of world-class motorsport.

Itinerary

  • Friday: Travel to Silverstone for the afternoon session and sprint race qualifying. Arrival mid-afternoon. Evening transfer to hotel for check-in and dinner.
  • Saturday: Breakfast, then head to Silverstone for Qualifying Day. Teams push cars to the limit to secure grid positions. Stay late to enjoy post-race entertainment before returning to the hotel.
  • Sunday: Race Day! After breakfast, travel to Silverstone for the main event. Witness the pinnacle of motorsport before returning to the hotel for your evening meal.
  • Monday: Enjoy a final breakfast before the comfortable coach journey home.

Choose Your Ticket Experience

General Admission

Freedom to explore. Choose your own spot on the grassy banks at legendary corners like Maggotts, Becketts, and Chapel. Bring a chair, watch the big screens, and soak up the electric atmosphere.

Vale Grandstand

Prime viewing at a critical braking zone. Watch drivers navigate the tricky final corners and Vale chicane. A fantastic spot for overtaking action and very close to the podium celebrations.

Woodcote A

Positioned at the exit of Luffield leading into the National Pit Straight. See the cars push to the limit accelerating out of the corner. Reserved seating with clear views of the high-speed action.

Important Ticket Info: Tickets are Mobile Entry only. You must have a smartphone and the Silverstone App to access your tickets.

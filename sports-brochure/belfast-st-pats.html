Belfast St Patrick's Celebrations

Belfast’s St. Patrick’s Day celebrations are a vibrant and exciting experience that showcase the city’s rich culture and lively spirit. Every year on March 17th, the streets come alive with festivities honouring Ireland’s patron saint. The highlight is the St. Patrick’s Day Parade, which winds through the city centre with a colorful display of floats, music, dancers, and performers.

The parade typically kicks off around noon, starting at Belfast City Hall. Following the parade, many of Belfast’s public spaces host free entertainment, including live concerts and street performances. The festivities continue in Belfast’s buzzing pubs and bars, where you can enjoy live Irish folk music, traditional food, and, of course, a pint of Guinness.

RHS Chelsea Flower Show

Step into the magical world of the Chelsea Flower Show, where every May, London's Royal Hospital Chelsea transforms into Britain's most prestigious garden festival. Discover breathtaking show gardens where top designers push the boundaries of creativity. The heart of the show, the magnificent Great Pavilion, feels like a floral wonderland, filled with spectacular displays from the country's finest nurseries. It's more than just a flower show – it's a celebration of the very best in British gardening.

Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew

Step into a world of stunning plants, glasshouses and peaceful landscapes at the Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew. With over 300 acres to explore, highlights include the famous Palm House, the treetop walkway, and beautifully kept gardens from all over the world. Whether you’re a keen gardener or just enjoy a relaxing day surrounded by nature, Kew is a true delight.

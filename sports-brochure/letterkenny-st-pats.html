Letterkenny St Patrick's Celebrations

Experience the magic of Ireland’s national holiday at Letterkenny's St. Patrick’s Day celebrations. Held annually on March 17th, the streets are transformed into a festive spectacle. The highlight is the vibrant parade featuring colourful floats, traditional Irish dancers, and marching bands.

Beyond the parade, Letterkenny hosts live street performances, craft fairs, and music sessions. Local pubs and restaurants join in the festivities with traditional Irish dishes and live music, ensuring the celebrations continue well into the evening.

Visit to Donegal

Donegal Town is a charming destination known for its scenic beauty and rich heritage. At the heart of the town is Donegal Castle, a 15th-century fortress. The town’s iconic Diamond is surrounded by quaint shops and traditional pubs. Combining natural beauty, history, and culture, Donegal Town offers something for every traveller.

The Allingham Arms Hotel, Bundoran

The Allingham Arms Hotel offers a warm welcome on Ireland’s stunning Atlantic coast. This popular four-star hotel is known for its friendly atmosphere, comfortable rooms, and excellent dining. Guests can enjoy hearty breakfasts and delicious evening meals before relaxing in the cosy lounge or taking in the lively entertainment.

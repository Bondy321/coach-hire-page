St Annes 1940's Event

Travel back in time as St Annes hosts its spectacular 1940s Weekend. The town transforms to celebrate the spirit of the wartime era, featuring historic vehicles, military parades, and authentic period costumes. Soak up the atmosphere with live music, dancing, and entertainment that brings the sights and sounds of the 1940s to life. Whether you are browsing the vintage stalls or watching the re-enactments, it is a nostalgic and vibrant experience for all ages.

Visit to Windermere

A short visit to Windermere offers a perfect escape into the heart of the Lake District, surrounded by breathtaking natural beauty. Windermere, England’s largest natural lake, is ideal for leisurely boat cruises, water sports, or simply enjoying a peaceful walk along its shores. The town itself is charming, with boutique shops, cafés, and traditional pubs offering a welcoming atmosphere. It is a perfect destination for a memorable short visit in one of the UK’s most picturesque regions.

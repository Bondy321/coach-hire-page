Option 1: Best Mate Enclosure

Experience the buzz of the Cheltenham Gold Cup from the Best Mate Enclosure. Located directly opposite the main Grandstand, this area offers excellent views of the racing and an electric atmosphere at great value. With easy access to food stalls, bars, and betting facilities, it’s a brilliant way to enjoy one of the biggest days in jump racing.

Option 2: Tattersalls Enclosure

Enjoy a step up in race day access with the Tattersalls Enclosure. From here, you’ll have entry to the paddock area to see the horses up close before and after each race, along with views of the winners’ enclosure and access to covered grandstand seating. It’s a great middle-ground option for racegoers wanting a bit more of the action without going full Club.

Option 3: Club Enclosure

For those looking for the full Cheltenham experience, the Club Enclosure offers the very best in race day access. Enjoy prime viewing spots, access to exclusive bars and restaurants, and full entry to the paddock and winners’ enclosure. It’s a premium experience with everything you need for a memorable day at one of the world’s most famous race meetings.

Holiday Inn Express Birmingham - Redditch

Situated just a short walk from Redditch Station, this hotel offers a modern and comfortable base. Rooms feature free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, walk-in showers, and a choice of pillows. The hotel features a restaurant serving evening meals, and a bar is available for guests to relax after a day out. With friendly service and a lift to all floors, it is an ideal spot for your Gold Cup break.

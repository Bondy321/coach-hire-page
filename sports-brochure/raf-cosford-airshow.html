RAF Cosford Airshow

Prepare for an unforgettable experience at the RAF Cosford Airshow, one of the UK’s premier aviation events! With entry included, enjoy a thrilling day of spectacular flying displays, historic aircraft, and impressive ground exhibitions showcasing the very best of military and civilian aviation.

From fast jets and vintage warbirds to the famous Red Arrows, it’s a day packed with excitement, atmosphere, and incredible photo opportunities – a real highlight for aviation enthusiasts and families alike.

Holiday Inn Express, Walsall

Enjoy a relaxing two-night dinner, bed & breakfast stay at the Holiday Inn Express, Walsall. This bright and modern hotel offers comfort and convenience throughout your stay. All bedrooms feature en-suite facilities, a TV, free Wi-Fi, and tea and coffee-making amenities - perfect for unwinding after a busy day.

A lift serves all floors for easy access, and the hotel’s friendly atmosphere makes it an ideal base for your weekend away. Dinner is served each evening in the hotel’s inviting restaurant, and breakfast is included each morning before setting off to enjoy the show.

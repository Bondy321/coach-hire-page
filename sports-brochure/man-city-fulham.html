Match Day: Man City v Fulham

Manchester City welcome Fulham to the Etihad in mid-February, aiming to keep their title charge on track. The champions haven’t lost to the Cottagers since 2009, but Marco Silva’s side arrive with nothing to lose and a reputation for playing brave, attacking football.

Fulham’s open style often makes for an eventful watch. With the likes of Haaland and De Bruyne set to feature, fans can expect a game full of movement, creativity, and chances at both ends.

The Etihad Stadium Tour

On Thursday we head back to the ground to immerse yourself in the City story. You will visit areas of the Stadium not usually available to the public. Highlights include:

  • Field questions with a virtual Pep Guardiola in the Press Room
  • Visit the Home Team dressing room with an audio-visual show
  • Walk down the famous glass players tunnel
  • Go Pitch-side for a view from the dug-outs
  • Walk through the award-winning Tunnel Club hospitality suite

With opportunities for photos and souvenirs, this tour offers a unique glimpse into the world of top-tier football.

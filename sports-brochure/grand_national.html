Option 1: Embankment Enclosure

Enjoy a relaxed atmosphere with a casual dress code in the Embankment enclosure. It features Europe's longest temporary bar, pop-up food villages, and a festive vibe. The famous viewing bank offers unparalleled views of the horses tackling the initial fences of the Randox Grand National.

Option 2: Festival Zone

For prime viewing of the closing stages, the Festival Zone is the place to be. Enjoy exclusive access to the Parade Ring and Winners’ Enclosure, plus live entertainment in the Red Rum Garden and Irish Bar. With big screens and betting facilities, you won't miss a moment of the action or the legendary atmosphere.

Visit to Liverpool

Experience the essence of Liverpool with a visit to the Albert Dock, the iconic Cavern Club, or the vibrant city centre. It’s the perfect opportunity to explore the city's rich cultural heritage, friendly atmosphere, and historic landmarks during your short getaway.

The Holiday Inn, Runcorn

The Holiday Inn Runcorn provides a modern, comfortable base for your break. Rooms feature free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and a pillow menu to help you unwind. A lift serves all floors, and with welcoming service and everything you need for a relaxing overnight stay, it is the perfect spot to recharge after a day at the races.

Grassington 1940's Event

Experience the charm and nostalgia of wartime Britain at the Grassington 1940s Weekend, a unique and immersive event set in the heart of the picturesque Yorkshire Dales. Held annually, this popular festival transforms the quaint village of Grassington into a vibrant recreation of life during the 1940s, offering an unforgettable day out for visitors of all ages.

The streets come alive with vintage vehicles, military reenactments, and live music from the era, including swing bands and jazz performances. Attendees are encouraged to dress in 1940s-style clothing, making you feel like you’ve truly stepped into a different time.

Visit to Windermere

Nestled in the stunning Lake District National Park, Windermere is the perfect destination for a short but unforgettable escape. Whether you're a nature lover or simply looking for a peaceful retreat, Windermere offers breathtaking landscapes, serene waters, and endless activities. Start your visit with a leisurely cruise on Lake Windermere, the largest natural lake in England, where you'll enjoy panoramic views of rolling hills and scenic shorelines.

Mercure Bradford, Bankfield Hotel

The hotel's elegant restaurant provides a welcoming setting for your evening meal and breakfast.

Trip Highlights

Beautiful Bath

Bath is made for a wander, with its honey-coloured Georgian streets, elegant crescents, and an unmistakable Jane Austen-era atmosphere. Take in some of the city’s best-known sights along the way, from the iconic Royal Crescent and The Circus to the grandeur of the Guildhall and Assembly Rooms.

Bridgerton Guided Tour

Add a fun twist with a guided walking tour to recognisable filming locations woven into the city centre! See spots used for the Modiste dress shop and Gunter’s tea shop, picking up behind-the-scenes stories as you explore Bath's most photogenic exteriors and stunning architecture.

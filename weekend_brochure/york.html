Trip Highlights

Historic York

Enjoy plenty of free time to uncover layers of history in this enchanting city. Wander the narrow, cobbled Shambles, marvel at the imposing Gothic architecture of York Minster, and absorb panoramic views from the intact city walls. York also boasts bustling markets, charming tea rooms, and a vibrant atmosphere.

Harrogate & Knaresborough

Discover Harrogate, renowned for its elegant spa heritage, the Royal Pump Room, beautiful Valley Gardens, and the stylish Montpellier Quarter. We also visit Knaresborough, a charming market town perched on a cliff, featuring medieval streets, historic shops, and stunning riverside views.

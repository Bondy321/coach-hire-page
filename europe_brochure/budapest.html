Trip Highlights

Storybook Prague

Step into a city of gothic spires and grand bridges. Soak up the atmosphere in the Old Town Square, watch the famous Astronomical Clock, and stroll across the iconic Charles Bridge towards the magnificent Prague Castle complex.

Imperial Vienna

Experience a true 'grand city' feel with imperial buildings and elegant café culture. Wander the historic centre to view St Stephen’s Cathedral, marvel at Schönbrunn Palace, or browse the lively food stalls of the Naschmarkt.

Beautiful Budapest

Discover the elegance of Pest and the hilltop views of Buda, split perfectly by the Danube. Take in the postcard views of the Hungarian Parliament, explore the fairytale Fisherman’s Bastion, and perhaps relax in the unique thermal baths.

Your Accommodation

Amedia Express Hotel, Prague

A modern, comfortable base in Prague’s Chodov area, located near the Metro station for easy access into the city centre. Rooms have a clean, contemporary feel with air conditioning and free Wi-Fi.

Dinner is served each night at The Columna Restaurant, a well-regarded local spot just a short walk away, known for a mix of classic Czech favourites and international-style dishes in a smart, relaxed setting.

H2 Hotel Wien Schönbrunn, Vienna

A fresh-feeling base in a really handy spot, located close to Schönbrunn Palace with easy access to the U-Bahn for exploring Vienna. The rooms are comfortable and practical, offering air conditioning and free Wi-Fi.

A standout feature is the H2 Hub, which works as the hotel’s sociable food-and-drink space, offering a generous breakfast buffet and a relaxed environment to unwind after sightseeing.

NH Collection Budapest City Centre

Set in a great location on the Pest side of the city, close to a major transport hub and within easy reach of Andrássy Avenue and the Hungarian State Opera.

The hotel features spacious, apartment-style suites with a seating area and free Wi-Fi. Guests can also choose to use the nearby Anantara New York Palace spa facilities (including a pool, sauna, and steam room) for a local charge.

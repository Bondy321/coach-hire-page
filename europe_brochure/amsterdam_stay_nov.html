Trip Highlights

Enchanting Amsterdam

Fall in love with the scenic canals, elegant narrow houses, and the classic atmosphere of the Jordaan and the Nine Streets, brimming with boutique shops and cosy cafes.

Discover world-class culture at Museumplein, home to the Rijksmuseum and Van Gogh Museum. Explore historic Dam Square or take a memorable, relaxing cruise along the iconic waterways.

Local Life

Hop on the easy-to-use trams and metros to explore. Wander the bustling Albert Cuyp Market for tasty Dutch street food, soaking up the vibrant local life at your own pace.

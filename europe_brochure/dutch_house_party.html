Trip Highlights

Valkenburg Charm

Discover old-world charm in hillier, greener Valkenburg. Explore the Castle Ruins, the famous Velvet Cave, or enjoy an easy wander along the River Geul.

Aachen & Monschau

Mix grand history at the striking Aachen Cathedral with the picture-perfect, half-timbered houses of Monschau, tucked beautifully into the Eifel hills.

Elegant Maastricht & Amsterdam

Enjoy the grand squares and lovely riverside atmosphere of Maastricht, plus an en-route visit to explore the iconic canals and vibrant culture of Amsterdam.

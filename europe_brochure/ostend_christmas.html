Trip Highlights

Brussels Winter Wonderland

Experience one of Europe's most enchanting festive events. The Grand Place sparkles with hundreds of wooden chalets, a towering Christmas tree, and the incredible scent of mulled wine and fresh waffles.

Fairytale Bruges

Enjoy an afternoon and evening excursion to Bruges. Stroll the Grote Markt, watch ice skaters under the iconic Belfry tower, and browse handcrafted gifts while listening to festive carolers.

Iconic Amsterdam

Enjoy a visit to the Netherlands' vibrant capital. Stroll through the charming streets of the Jordaan district, explore world-class museums, or soak up the lively ambiance in one of the city’s many cafés.

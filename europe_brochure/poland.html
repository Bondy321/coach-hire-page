Trip Highlights

Historic Kraków

Discover one of Poland’s most captivating cities, featuring a beautifully preserved Old Town, grand medieval squares, and the famous Renaissance Cloth Hall. Wander the historic streets, visit the royal Wawel Castle, or explore the atmospheric Kazimierz district.

Auschwitz-Birkenau

A deeply moving guided visit to the largest of the German Nazi concentration and extermination camps. Now a UNESCO World Heritage Site, it stands as a vital and significant place of remembrance, education, and reflection.

Wieliczka Salt Mine

Journey deep underground into a remarkable world of salt-carved chambers, winding passageways, and subterranean lakes. The absolute highlight is the breathtaking Chapel of St Kinga, a vast underground cathedral carved entirely from rock salt.

Zakopane & Dunajec Gorge

Sitting at the foot of the Tatra Mountains, Zakopane offers unique highland culture and dramatic peaks. Enjoy a scenic rafting experience down the Dunajec River Gorge, drifting peacefully on a traditional wooden raft past steep cliffs and forested slopes.

Your Accommodation

Hotel Wyspiański, Kraków

A comfortable 3-star hotel in the heart of Kraków, well placed for enjoying the city’s historic centre. The famous Main Market Square is just a short walk away, with the Planty Park, cafés, restaurants, and many of Kraków’s best-known sights close by.

The hotel offers bright, simple and welcoming accommodation, with rooms featuring en-suite facilities, Wi-Fi, TV, and useful in-room comforts. It features a lift, 24-hour reception, a restaurant, and a bar, making it a highly practical and convenient base.

Nosalowy Park & Spa Hotel, Zakopane

An elegant 5-star hotel in the centre of Zakopane, set within a peaceful beech park just a short walk from the famous Krupówki Street. It combines historic architecture with a stylish modern wing inspired by Zakopane Art Nouveau and Art Deco design.

Rooms are finished to a high standard with comfortable furnishings, Wi-Fi, and bathrobes. The hotel also boasts a high-quality restaurant, bar, indoor swimming pool, wellness area, saunas, and fitness facilities, giving it a real sense of luxury during your stay.

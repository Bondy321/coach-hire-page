Trip Highlights

Medieval Bruges

Immerse yourself in the "Venice of the North". Stroll the bustling Grote Markt, view the magnificent Belfry Tower, and discover world-famous chocolate shops alongside romantic, picturesque canals.

Bustling Brussels

Enjoy a visit to Belgium's capital. Marvel at the grand guildhalls of the Grand Place, window-shop in elegant arcades, and treat yourself to classic Belgian waffles.

Enchanting Amsterdam

Discover Amsterdam's iconic waterways and historic architecture. Wander the lively Jordaan district, relax by the canals, and soak up the vibrant Dutch culture at your own pace.

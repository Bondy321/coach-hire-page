Highlights & Itinerary

NorthLink Ferries

Enjoy an overnight journey from Aberdeen to Lerwick. Choose from well-appointed cabins or relax in the ship's lounges and restaurant, arriving refreshed in Shetland.

Brae Hotel, Shetland

A warm, welcoming retreat in the village of Brae, 30 mins from Lerwick. Offers comfortable, modern rooms and an on-site restaurant and bar serving local produce.

Guided Shetland Tours

Enjoy two full-day guided tours. Journey to Unst, the UK's northernmost point, visiting a replica Viking longship. Meet famous Shetland ponies, and discover 5,000 years of history at Jarlshof.

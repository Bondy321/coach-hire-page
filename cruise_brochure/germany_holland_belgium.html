Dutch Winter Wonderland

December transforms Amsterdam into pure magic. Discover picture-perfect canals lined with twinkling lights, cosy brown cafés glowing with warmth, and vibrant squares filled with ice rinks and festive treats.

Your 7-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Embark in London Tilbury

Following your luxury overnight coach travel, arrive in London Tilbury to board the impressive Ambition. Settle into your cabin and enjoy your first evening on board as we set sail.

Day 2: At Sea

A peaceful day at sea to relax into ship life. Treat yourself in the Green Sea Spa, enjoy the comfortable lounges, or dive into the daytime activities programme.

Day 3: Hamburg, Germany

Arrive in Germany's coolest port city. Wander through sparkling cobblestone streets, browse the incredible festive markets, and enjoy warming mugs of traditional mulled wine.

Day 4: At Sea

Enjoy another relaxing day onboard. Make the most of the ship's full-board dining, take a dip in the pool, and prepare for a spectacular evening of live entertainment.

Day 5: Ijmuiden (for Amsterdam)

Dock in Ijmuiden. Choose to explore the bustling harbour, or take an optional transfer to Amsterdam to discover illuminated waterways, winter ice rinks, and cosy cafés.

Day 6: Zeebrugge (for Bruges)

Discover Belgium's picturesque coast, or take an optional transfer to medieval Bruges. Experience twinkling Christmas markets, Gothic architecture, and world-famous Belgian chocolate.

Day 7: Disembark in Tilbury

After a final breakfast, arrive back into London Tilbury and disembark. Rejoin your coach for the comfortable journey home, bringing your festive escape to a close.

Your 9-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Embark in Newcastle

Travel to Newcastle Tyne to board your ship. Settle into your cabin, explore the decks, and enjoy your first evening on board as you sail away towards Norway.

Day 2: At Sea

A peaceful day at sea gives you time to relax into ship life. Enjoy the lounges, restaurants, entertainment, and open-deck views as we cruise north.

Day 3: Alesund

Arrive in Alesund, famous for its elegant Art Nouveau architecture and sweeping views. Wander the waterfront and enjoy the easygoing Nordic atmosphere.

Day 4: Maloy

Nestled on Norway’s rugged west coast, Maloy offers a taste of traditional coastal life. Take in the scenic harbour and the calm beauty of this quiet maritime town.

Day 5: Flam, Sognefjord & Aurlandsfjord

Sail through some of Norway’s most spectacular scenery as we visit Flam and cruise the dramatic Sognefjord and Aurlandsfjord. A true highlight of the voyage.

Day 6: Austefjorden (for Bergen)

Arrive in Austefjorden, the gateway to Bergen. Known for its colourful wooden houses and deep cultural charm, Bergen offers plenty to discover at your own pace.

Day 7: Haugesund

Haugesund blends coastal scenery with rich Viking heritage. Explore the seafront, enjoy local cafes, or visit ancient sites linked to Norway’s early kings.

Day 8: At Sea

Your final day at sea is a chance to unwind, revisit your favourite spots on the ship, and enjoy the entertainment on board as we make our way back towards the UK.

Day 9: Disembark in Newcastle

On arrival back in Newcastle Tyne, you’ll disembark and rejoin your coach for the comfortable journey home, bringing your Norwegian adventure to a close.

Your 20-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Embark in Dundee

Travel to Dundee by coach, board the impressive Ambition, and settle into your cabin as we set sail out from the River Tay.

Days 2 - 4: At Sea

Settle into ship life. Explore the lounges, enjoy the daytime activities, relax in the Green Sea Spa, and take in the spectacular evening theatre shows.

Day 5: Leixões (for Porto), Portugal

Dock in Leixões. Head into Porto to wander the historic Ribeira riverside and view the Dom Luís I Bridge, or stay local and enjoy the beaches of Matosinhos.

Day 6: At Sea

A perfect day to learn something new with informative guest speakers, or join the ORCA team on deck for guided wildlife watching.

Day 7: Gibraltar & The Solar Eclipse!

A truly monumental day. Witness a breathtaking Total Solar Eclipse from the ship! Afterwards, head up the Upper Rock to see the famous macaques and St Michael’s Cave.

Day 8: Almeria, Spain

Explore dramatic landscapes. Visit the Moorish Alcazaba fortress perched above the city, or venture inland to the cinematic Tabernas Desert.

Day 9: Alicante, Spain

Stroll the palm-lined Explanada, wander the old town lanes, and take in sweeping coastal views from the magnificent Santa Bárbara Castle.

Day 10: Valencia, Spain

Choose between the historic Cathedral and Central Market, or marvel at the futuristic, awe-inspiring architecture of the City of Arts and Sciences.

Day 11: Ibiza, Spain

Make it lively or laid-back. Explore the UNESCO-listed Dalt Vila (old town) with its cobbled lanes, or relax by the chic waterfront marina.

Day 12: At Sea

After a run of exciting ports, enjoy a slower pace. Relax on the pool deck, visit the library, or join a craft studio session.

Day 13: Ceuta, North Africa

Discover where two continents meet! Dive into the fascinating cultural mix at Plaza de Africa and explore the historic Royal Walls.

Day 14: Cadiz, Spain

Wander the charming plazas and sun-drenched beaches of this ancient seaport, soaking up its deep Andalusian allure.

Day 15: At Sea

Enjoy complete freedom. Browse the onboard shops, bounce between lounges, and lean into the live music and entertainment in the evening.

Day 16: Vigo (for Santiago de Compostela)

Nestled in Spain's Galician region, explore Vigo's historic Old Town and Castro Fortress, or savour fresh, world-class local seafood.

Days 17 - 19: At Sea

Your final relaxing days at sea. Revisit your favourite dining spots, join a final ORCA watch, and soak up the buzzing farewell atmosphere in the lounges.

Day 20: Disembark in Dundee

After a final breakfast, disembark the Ambition in Dundee. Rejoin your coach for the comfortable journey home, taking with you unforgettable memories.

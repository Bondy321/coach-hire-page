Your 10-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Embark in Dundee

Travel to Dundee by coach, board the Ambition, and settle into your cabin. Explore the ship's lounges and enjoy your first evening dinner as we set sail.

Day 2: At Sea

Take the day to familiarize yourself with the ship. Enjoy the spa, relax in the library, or dive into the daytime activities programme.

Day 3: Ijmuiden (for Amsterdam)

Explore the bustling harbour of Ijmuiden, or take an optional transfer to Amsterdam to wander its iconic canals, historic Jordaan district, and world-class museums.

Day 4: Zeebrugge (for Bruges)

Discover Belgium's maritime history. Visit the historic port, savour local cuisine at seaside cafes, or head inland to the medieval charm of Bruges.

Day 5: Honfleur, France

Wander one of Normandy's prettiest harbour towns. Explore narrow streets, colourful buildings, and art galleries, or book an optional excursion to Claude Monet’s House & Gardens.

Day 6: At Sea

After a busy few days exploring Europe, relax onboard. Take in an informative guest speaker session and enjoy elegant dining in the evening.

Day 7: Brest, France

Located on the briny coast of Brittany. Stroll along the quaysides, discover historic fortifications, and enjoy authentic Breton cuisine.

Day 8: Torquay, England

A classic English Riviera gem. Explore palm-lined promenades, sandy beaches, Agatha Christie’s heritage, and the vibrant seaside atmosphere of this Devon resort.

Day 9: At Sea

Your final full day at sea. Soak up the ship's atmosphere, perhaps treat yourself to speciality dining, and enjoy a lively farewell evening in the theatre.

Day 10: Disembark in Dundee

Arrive back into Dundee after breakfast. Disembark and rejoin your coach for the journey home, bringing a wonderful coastal voyage to a close.

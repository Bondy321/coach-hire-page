Your 12-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Embark in Dundee

Travel to Dundee Cruise Terminal to board the Ambition. Settle into your cabin and enjoy your first evening at sea as the waterfront fades behind you.

Day 2: At Sea

Get into the rhythm of the ship. Relax with a coffee, enjoy the daytime activities, and join the ORCA team on deck for your first guided wildlife-spotting moments.

Day 3: Klaksvik, Faroe Islands

Enjoy a breathtaking setting between two back-to-back inlets. Immerse yourself in Faroese history, architecture, and gastronomy in the islands' second-largest town.

Day 4: Runavik, Faroe Islands

A lively harbour town offering a lovely introduction to the Faroes. Stroll the colourful waterfront or head out across Eysturoy to view striking, dramatic gorges.

Day 5: At Sea

A day for wellness and relaxation. Start with a workout in the gym, a swim in the pool, or head to the Green Sea Spa for a peaceful switch-off.

Day 6: Reykjavik, Iceland

Discover Iceland's vibrant capital. View Hallgrimskirkja, the Harpa Concert Hall, and the Sun Voyager sculpture, or head out from the old harbour on a whale watching trip.

Day 7: Isafjordur, Iceland

Explore the harbour and Westfjords Heritage Museum, take in the views from the Troll Seat, or visit the famous multi-tier Dynjandi waterfall.

Day 8: Akureyri, Iceland

A relaxed waterfront town on the Eyjafjordur fjord. Visit the colorful Botanical Gardens or head straight out into the fjord to spot humpback whales.

Day 9: Seydisfjordur, Iceland

Wander the famous rainbow-painted street leading to the blue church, explore local craft shops, or visit the nearby Gufufoss waterfall for a classic Icelandic moment.

Day 10: At Sea

Enjoy a slow morning and a long lunch in one of the dining venues, before an evening of live shows and performances in the theatre.

Day 11: At Sea

Your final sea day. Join a last ORCA deck watch to spot wildlife, or simply find a quiet spot to read before the final night's lively entertainment.

Day 12: Disembark in Dundee

After breakfast, arrive back into Dundee. Disembark and rejoin your coach for the comfortable journey home, taking incredible memories with you.

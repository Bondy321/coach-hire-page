Your 14-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Embark in Dundee

Travel to Dundee to board your ship. Settle into your cabin, explore the decks, and enjoy your first evening on board as we sail into the North Sea.

Day 2: At Sea

A peaceful day to relax into ship life. Attend the first astronomy talks, enjoy the lounges, and join the ORCA team on deck for wildlife spotting.

Day 3: Runavik, Faroe Islands

Experience the lived-in charm of this Faroese harbour town. Stroll the waterfront or head out to explore the dramatic mountain views and moorlands of Eysturoy.

Day 4: At Sea

A day of anticipation and preparation. Relax in the Green Sea Spa or pools, and join the expert speakers as they build excitement for tomorrow's celestial event.

Day 5: Total Solar Eclipse (At Sea)

The headline moment! Positioned in remote Arctic waters off East Greenland, witness the awe-inspiring total solar eclipse from the open decks with live expert commentary.

Day 6: At Sea

Reflect on the eclipse over a relaxed sea day. As evening falls, the Perseid meteor shower reaches its peak, offering spectacular stargazing opportunities from the dark northern waters.

Day 7: Reykjavik, Iceland

Discover Iceland's vibrant capital. View the striking Hallgrimskirkja, explore the Old Harbour area, or embark on an unforgettable whale-watching trip.

Day 8: Grundarfjordur, Iceland

Arrive in a town with a truly cinematic setting right beside Kirkjufell, one of Iceland's most famous mountains. Take in the sweeping views or explore the wider Snaefellsnes region.

Day 9: At Sea

Enjoy the rhythm of the ship. Make the most of the full-board dining, browse the shops, and prepare for another potential evening of brilliant stargazing on deck.

Day 10: Seydisfjordur, Iceland

Tucked at the end of a narrow fjord, explore this colourful, creative town. View the famous Blue Church or take a short trip to see the roaring Gufufoss waterfall.

Day 11: At Sea

Your final full sea day before reaching Scotland. Soak up the atmosphere, enjoy a long lunch, and take in a spectacular theatre show in the evening.

Day 12: Lerwick, Shetland Isles

Round off the cruise in Shetland's proudly local capital. Enjoy the bustling harbour, historic waterfront, and deep seafaring heritage before heading south.

Day 13: At Sea

A final day to unwind. Revisit your favourite spots, join a last ORCA deck watch, and enjoy the buzzing farewell atmosphere during the Captain's Gala events.

Day 14: Disembark in Dundee

After breakfast, arrive back into Dundee and disembark. Rejoin your coach for the comfortable journey home, taking incredible celestial and coastal memories with you.

Your 11-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Embark in Dundee

Travel to Dundee by luxury coach and board the impressive Ambition. Settle into your cabin as we begin our spectacular journey north to the fjords.

Day 2: At Sea

Settle into ship life. Take time to explore the lounges, enjoy the daytime activities programme, and relax into the rhythm of life at sea.

Day 3: Scenic Cruising of Lysefjord

Awe-inspiring scenery that must be seen to be believed. Glide past towering cliffs and look out for the famous plateau of Pulpit Rock rising 2,000 feet above the water.

Day 4: Haugesund

Explore coastal character and Viking history. Visit the Haraldshaugen monument, stroll the harbour, or head out to view the magnificent Langfoss waterfall.

Day 5: Eidfjord

Sail through stunning landscapes of cascading waterfalls and steep cliffs before docking in charming Eidfjord, a peaceful village surrounded by dramatic mountains.

Day 6: At Sea

A perfect day to unwind. Relax in the Green Sea Spa, take a dip in the pool, or find a quiet spot to read before an evening of excellent dining and entertainment.

Day 7: Flåm, Sognefjord & Aurlandsfjord

Cruise through postcard-perfect scenery to Flåm. Take the famous Flåm Railway, visit the Stegastein viewpoint, or simply enjoy a coffee by the still water.

Day 8: Austefjorden (for Bergen)

Blending nature, history, and authentic Norwegian life. Visit the WWII sites at Telavåg, stroll through Glesvær, or learn about local fishing traditions.

Day 9: Sandnes & Cruise Boknafjord

Enjoy an easy wander through friendly Sandnes or hike Dalsnuten for cracking views. Later, spend time on deck scenic cruising through the wide, island-dotted Boknafjord.

Day 10: At Sea

Your final full day to soak up the ship's atmosphere. Enjoy a long lunch, browse the shops, and save some energy for a lively final night of entertainment.

Day 11: Disembark in Dundee

After breakfast, arrive back into Dundee to disembark. Rejoin your coach for the journey home, taking with you unforgettable memories of Norway.

Trip Highlights

Titanic Quarter

Explore the world-famous Titanic Belfast Museum and learn about the city’s shipbuilding legacy. Take a scenic walk along the River Lagan to see the SS Nomadic, the last remaining White Star Line vessel.

History & Heritage

Delve into the city's past with a visit to the Peace Wall and the famous murals of Falls and Shankill Road. Don't miss the Crumlin Road Gaol, a former prison turned fascinating museum.

City Culture & Nightlife

Soak up the atmosphere at St. George's Market or the bustling Cathedral Quarter. Enjoy a pint at The Crown Liquor Saloon, a beautifully preserved Victorian pub, or catch a show at the Grand Opera House.

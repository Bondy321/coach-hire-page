Trip Highlights

Donegal Town

Enjoy free time wandering the traditional streets and colourful shopfronts of this warm, welcoming town. It's the perfect place to browse for gifts, pick up local knitwear, or relax in a cosy café.

The Wild Atlantic Way

Take in big coastal views and rolling scenery along Ireland’s most atmospheric seascapes. Enjoy a scenic drive with memorable stops at Rosses Point and Sligo before continuing to Enniscrone.

Historic Derry

Explore Derry on foot, taking in the sweeping views from the historic City Walls. Cross the beautiful Peace Bridge, view the Guildhall, or reflect on the city's story at the famous Bogside murals.

Trip Highlights

Vibrant Dublin (Full Day)

Explore Ireland's bustling capital. Visit iconic sights like Trinity College, wander the cobbled streets of the Temple Bar district, or take in the panoramic views at the Guinness Storehouse.

Strokestown Park House & Gardens

Step back in time at this beautifully restored 18th-century Georgian mansion. Explore the tranquil walled gardens and discover moving insights at the National Famine Museum located on the estate.

Charming Galway (Full Day)

Experience the lively atmosphere of the Latin Quarter, stroll along the Salthill Promenade for stunning views of Galway Bay, and soak in the city's uniquely warm, welcoming spirit.

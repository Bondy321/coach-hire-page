Trip Highlights

Vibrant Dublin

Explore Ireland's lively capital. Cross the Ha'penny Bridge, wander through Temple Bar, or visit iconic sights like Trinity College and the Guinness Storehouse.

Historic Waterford & Wexford

Discover Waterford's Viking Triangle and Reginald's Tower, then enjoy the relaxed coastal charm of Wexford, watching the boats as the River Slaney meets the sea.

Medieval Kilkenny

Marvel at the impressive Kilkenny Castle, walk the city's famous Medieval Mile, or take in the sweeping views from the top of St Canice's Cathedral round tower.
